Who we are

Based in Zagreb, Croatia, Champions of Doom airsoft team conducts Mil-Sim style of airsoft activites, focusing on team play and coordinated efforts.

We strive to always improve our skills and grow as a team, through team training sessions, friendly skirmishes with other teams and participation in big Mil-Sim events.
